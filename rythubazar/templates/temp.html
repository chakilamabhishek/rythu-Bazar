About

A lot of cases have been observed where the farmers do not get the right amount paid to them for the crops which are produced by them.

Our portal ensures a hassle-free service to connect the consumers directly to the farmers all across India.

Buyers

Buyers get organic fruits, grains and vegetables directly from farmlands thus eliminating third-party vendors who sell them at a higher price with a lesser quality.

Farmers

Farmers can easily put the information about the various crops they offer along with the information about price and quality.
